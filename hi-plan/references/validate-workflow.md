# Validate Workflow

Critical questions interview to validate plan assumptions.

## Step 1: Read Plan
Read plan.md + phase-*.md. Look for assumptions, risks, tradeoffs.

## Step 2: Generate Questions
Focus on: assumptions, risks, tradeoffs, architecture.
Mark recommended option per question.

## Step 3: Interview
AskUserQuestion - group related questions (max 4/call).

## Step 4: Document
Add ## Validation Log to plan.md with answers.

## Step 5: Propagate
Update affected phase files with validation decisions.

## Output
- Questions asked count
- Decisions confirmed
- Phase propagation results
- Recommendation: proceed or revise
